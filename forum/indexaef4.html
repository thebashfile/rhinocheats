Hello, today I will be reviewing the cheat for mw2
aimbot 10/10: The aimbot is very good and solid. No fps drops or ping drops what's so ever. I like the fact that the developers included silent aim as not a whole lot of hacks have this. You also have fov aimbot so you can either rage or play legit.

esp 10/10: the esp is very good and supports thousands of different colors. You have team esp enemy esp and much more. Included also is charms. This makes this cheat defiently feel like a premium product unlike other hacks out there.

menu 10/10: The menu is highly customizable and you can change font, colors and even the background not to mention its a mouse menu instead of use keys which makes things a whole lot easier.


misc features 7/10: Fov changer is working fine however the chat spammer keeps disconnecting me, I hope the staff can fix this problem.

host features ??? /10: haven't been able to try this out since I'm vac banned so I hope the developers can add a way to host while being ening vac banned.

price 6.5/10
There are many different price options for you to choose from such as one day or lifetime. I gave some points for the lifetime feature since not a whole lot of cheat developers have this option but some points have to be taken away because $65 for a hack for a 9 year old game is a bit too much. I would suggest lowing it to about $50-$55 instead.

Overalls 9.5/10
Overall it's a good and stable hack, but the price is just a bit too high. If anyone is interested and has the money this is a very good hack to get.
